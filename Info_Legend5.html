Ebot

What does one thing that a human has and a robot didn't? Showing emotions. As a robot, Ebot could show his emotions through a big screen in his chest. So, is he a human? I guess we could call him sort of a human since he can show his emotions. Those emotions that he shows on the screen indicate his behavior. For example, if there is a red grumpy face on the screen, you better watch out because he is in combat mode. With a special zipline on his shoulder, there is not a single thing that he can't chase.
