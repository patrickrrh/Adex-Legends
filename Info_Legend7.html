Stormful

Since high school, Samantha was a shy and coward girl. But, suddenly, all of those personalities were gone in a blink of an eye because of a single incident. When she was walking home from school, she got struck by lightning, and she became an irritable girl. Since then, she got a tremendous power, where she can shoot lightning from her hand. Also, people don't recognize her as Samantha again. Since then, she has been called "Stormful".
