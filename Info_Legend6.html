Heycrow

Heycrow is a professional hunter in the Land of ADEX. With the help of his crow "Spencer", he could find anyone that he is looking for from miles away. The special thing about his ability is he never use any kind of modern technology. He never use them because since he was a little kid, his parent always taught him to use everything that has been provided by the nature, plus his senses. Can you imagine if he is equipped with modern hunting tech? Would he become unstoppable? I guess it will become a story for another future.
