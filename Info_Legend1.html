Baltimore

Since he was a little kid, Baltimore A.K.A Dr. Spencer is always into science. Then, his dream came true when he became a world-known scientist. His invention of a healing gas that could cure a lot of diseases is the achievement that made him a world-known scientist. But, when a group of people came and stole his work, he is not the same as Dr. Spencer again. Since then, the world has known him as Baltimore. A fearless scientist with a deadly gas that always targetting all bad guys.
