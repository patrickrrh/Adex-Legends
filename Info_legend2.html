Bullets

We can agree that war is a scary situation, but not for Bullets. He is a very accurate shooter that can shoot in any condition. From the hot and dazzling Sahara to the cold and blurry Antarctica. It makes him the most dangerous person in the world with a gun. On top of that, he also has a very strong metal shield. For him, a shield and a gun are enough to fight anything in the world.
