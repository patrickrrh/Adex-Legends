Droner

Droner is a cheerful and kind girl, yet she can be very dangerous. In the Land of ADEX, where everything is dangerous, she still shows her kindness by helping other people. This form of help came from her drone, which contain a health pack to heal others and herself. That drone could also be used as a weapon if she needed to. So, don't get fooled by her cheerful and kind look, because she can be a very dangerous threat.
